CRUELTY BY HUSBAND OR RELATIVES

Domestic violence in India is endemic. Around 70% of women in India are victims of domestic violence, according to Renuka Chowdhury, former Union minister for Women and Child Development.The National Crime Records Bureau reveal that a crime against a woman is committed every three minutes, a woman is raped every 29 minutes, a dowry death occurs every 77 minutes, and one case of cruelty committed by either the husband or relative of the husband occurs every nine minutes.This occurs despite the fact that women in India are legally protected from domestic abuse under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act.

The following graph shows the states with maximum number of cruelty by husband or relatives cases reported. As per the data by National Bureau of crime West Bengal,Andhra Pradesh,Rajasthan,Maharashtra can be considered a threatful place for women but the higher figures are possibly because of the large population in these states.


No of cruelty by husband or relatives per 100000 Women indicate that Rajasthan,Andhra Pradesh have a similar number of cases from 2011 -2015 while state of Assam and West Bengal having a very high increases in the cases registered. Kerala being one of most literate state in India has also high rate of cruelty even though it has declined over the time.Assam has been on the continuous rise and got highest cases for 2014 & 2015.

Comparing the total number of cases and number of cases per 100000 women bring to us different states like Kerala and Tripura.This graph let's us see how state with less number of cases can be dangerous as the population plays a greater role in the determination of hotspots. It is observed that Assam,West Bengal,Rajasthan,Andhra Pradesh has significant figure in both the list making it a major hotspot for rape cases in India.Delhi ,Tripura,Kerala have very less relative value in no of cases with other states but are a hotspot of crime when female population is taken into consideration and an opposite could be seen in case of Uttar pradesh where the figures get very low.
