RAPE

Rape is the third most common crime against women in India.India has reported maximum of 36735 crimes in an year in 2014 , Out of these most were committed by parents/family, relatives, neighbors and other known persons implying that , men known to the victim committed 98 per cent of reported rapes. It is expected by that the number of unreported cases of rape will bring the number even higher.

Sudden increase in number of cases reported after 2012 could be because of growing awareness among the masses after the Nirbhaya case and also because of the inability of government to take an action against the cases leading lesser fear among the culprits/potential culprits.

The following graph shows the states with maximum number of rape cases reported. As per the data by National Bureau of crime, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh can be considered a threatful place for women but the higher figures are possibly because of the large population in these states.

No of rapes per 100000 Women indicate that North eastern states like Assam, Mizoram, Tripura have the maximum rape intensity in India with Mizoram being on the top in 2011-2012 with significant higher value from 2013-2015. Delhi has the maximum rape intensity from 2013-2015. Chandigarh also making to the list with high figures.

Comparing the total number of cases and number of cases per 100000 women bring to us different states.This graph let's us see how state with less number of cases can be dangerous as the population plays a greater role in the determination of hotspots. It is observed that Uttar pradesh has significant figure in both the list making it a major hotspot for rape cases in India followed by Delhi and North eastern states.
