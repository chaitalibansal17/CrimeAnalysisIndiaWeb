What's important is to consider the severity/seriousness of these crimes and find a safest place for women.


Severity Index is a method to add weights to each crime category on the basis of it's seriousness which is determined by the level of punishment,the effect on the victim and the fear among people. Crimes are usually classified as verbal abuse, Non-verbal abuse, Physical abuse, serious physical abuse and others. Where verbal abuse involves Catcalls, Whistling, Commenting, Sexual Invites and non verbal include ogling, staring, taking pictures, Indecent Exposure.

The weighting system used is to assign levels of seriousness to each offence.Physical abuse are more serious than verbal. Hence will be assigned a higher weight.

We have added weights to the major crimes in India as per the severity index and crime classification.


OFFENCE WEIGHT
RAPE 2.2
DOWRY DEATH 1.8
KIDNAPPING AND ABDUCTION 1.5
TRAFFICKING 1.5
CRUELTY BY HUSBAND OR RELATIVES 1.2
SEXUAL HARASSMENT AND MOLESTATION 1.2
INDECENT REPRESENTATION OF WOMEN 0.6

■ Rape and dowry death are given the highest weight because they are severe physical Abuse.
■ Kidnapping , abduction and trafficking are the same level of physical offence.
■ Cruelty, sexual harassment and molestation are verbal as well as physical abuse taking lesser weight than above offences.
■ Indecent representation of women in Movies, images, videos over the internet is non verbal abuse. Therefore, it is given least weight.


Our model here is to multiply crime rate of every offence with it's weight for every state and determine it's total Score. Crime rate is the number of crimes reported upon the female population into 100000.We have choosen crime rate over number of crimes reported because it is a better indicator as we have noticed in analysis of each crime type in analysis section.
